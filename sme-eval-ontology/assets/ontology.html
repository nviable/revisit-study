Ontology Structure

Digital Visual Media Forensics Ontology

This ontology organizes terminology from 273 research papers in digital Media forensics into four main categories:

  • Target Modality: Types of media content analyzed
  • Forensic Goal & Task: Types of manipulations and alterations detected
  • Features & Cues: Technical features used for detection
  • Search Scope: Analysis scope and target areas

Click on any category to explore the hierarchy and see detailed information about terms and their relationships.
