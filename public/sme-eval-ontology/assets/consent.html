Consent to Participate in Research

Study Title

SME Evaluation Survey: Digital Visual Media Forensics Ontology

Introduction

You are being asked to participate in a research study. This form provides you with information about the study. The Principal Investigator (the person in charge of this research) or his/her representative will also describe this study to you and answer all of your questions. Please read the information below and ask questions about anything you do not understand before deciding whether or not to take part.

Purpose

The purpose of this study is to evaluate the structure, completeness, and utility of a Digital Visual Media Forensics ontology. Your feedback will help improve the ontology and make it more useful for the digital forensics community.

Procedures

If you agree to participate, you will be asked to:

  1. Complete a survey about your expertise in digital media forensics
  2. Evaluate different aspects of the ontology through a series of questions
  3. Provide feedback on the interactive viewer
  4. Share your overall assessment of the ontology

The survey should take approximately 25-35 minutes to complete.

Risks

There are no known risks associated with participating in this study.

Benefits

Your participation will help improve the Digital Visual Media Forensics ontology, which aims to standardize terminology and structure in the field. This could benefit the entire digital forensics community.

Confidentiality

Your responses will be kept confidential. All data will be stored securely and will only be used for research purposes. Your name and any identifying information will not be included in any reports or publications.

Voluntary Participation

Your participation in this study is completely voluntary. You may choose not to participate or to withdraw from the study at any time without penalty.
